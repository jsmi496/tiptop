Repair

Roof Repair

If your roof is leaking, missing shingles, showing storm damage, or causing water stains inside your home, our team can inspect the issue and recommend the right repair.

Is Your Roof Telling You Something?

These are the most common warning signs that your roof needs attention. If you're seeing any of these, a free inspection is the right first step.

🏠

Missing or Damaged Shingles

What it may mean: Wind, age, or storm damage

💧

Water Stains on Ceilings

What it may mean: Possible roof leak

🥱

Mold or Mildew in the Attic

What it may mean: Moisture or ventilation issue

📈

Higher Energy Bills

What it may mean: Poor insulation or roof ventilation

🌧️

Dripping Sounds During Rain

What it may mean: Active leak

🌠

Drafts or Temperature Changes

What it may mean: Roofing or attic performance issue

Seeing any of these signs? Don't wait — early action prevents small problems from becoming expensive ones.
