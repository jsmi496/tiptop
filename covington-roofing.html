Know the Signs

How Do You Know If Your
Roof Needs Repair?

Catching problems early saves you money and protects your home. Here are the warning signs Covington homeowners should never ignore.

01

Your roof hasn't been replaced or repaired in over 20 years

02

Water stains or discoloration on interior walls or ceilings

03

Moss or mildew growing on the roof surface

04

Gutters that are clogged, sagging, or damaged

05

Missing, cracked, or curling shingles

06

Visible sagging in any area of the roof

07

Dark streaks on interior walls or ceilings

08

Loose material around the chimney or flashing

09

Daylight visible through the roof or attic

If you've noticed any of these issues, don't wait for the problem to get worse. Give Tip-Top Roofing a call — we'll inspect your roof and give you an honest assessment of what needs to be done.
